Real-World Uses: Provenance, Purpose & Authenticity

arcHIVE is built for those who preserve, protect, and publish the truth. Whether you're archiving oral histories or capturing real-time video evidence, arcHIVE gives you cryptographic proof and decentralized permanence.

  • 🕒 Time-stamping Evidence – Record events with tamper-proof cryptographic signatures.
  • 🗂️ Provenance Tracking – Maintain transparent origin chains for historical and creative content.
  • 📸 Authenticating Media – Bind photos and video to a verifiable blockchain identity.
  • 🔐 Chain-of-Custody Assurance – Securely log transfers and modifications for critical documentation.
  • 🧾 Decentralized Proof of Existence – Demonstrate content creation without revealing private details.
  • 🎙️ Preserving Testimonies – Capture oral histories and eyewitness accounts with immutable timestamps.

If you care about what really happened — arcHIVE was built for you.
