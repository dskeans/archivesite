Who It's For

arcHIVE is built for those who preserve, protect, and publish the truth. Whether you're archiving oral histories or capturing real-time video evidence, arcHIVE gives you cryptographic proof and decentralized permanence.

  • 📰 Journalists – to timestamp interviews, verify footage, and protect sources.
  • 📚 Librarians & Archivists – to safeguard digital collections with blockchain verifiability.
  • 🎥 Creators & Documentarians – to own and authenticate their original media.
  • 🏛️ Museum Curators – to provide verifiable provenance for digital exhibits.
  • 📂 Evidence Custodians – to ensure chain-of-custody and media integrity.
  • 🎙️ Educators & Historians – to preserve first-person accounts in immutable form.

If you care about what really happened — arcHIVE was built for you.
