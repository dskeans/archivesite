ARCHIVE

arcHIVE is a decentralized platform for capturing, verifying, and tokenizing your most important audiovisual documents. With blockchain-backed authenticity, IPFS storage, and a built-in camera UI, your media stays secure, traceable, and future-proof.

Who It's For

arcHIVE is built for those who preserve, protect, and publish the truth. Whether you're archiving oral histories or capturing real-time video evidence, arcHIVE gives you cryptographic proof and decentralized permanence.
