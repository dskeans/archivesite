Vision and Purpose

arcHIVE is a blockchain-powered recording platform that captures, verifies, and archives moments that matter. With our DocuMINT module, every photo or video is timestamped, hashed, stored on IPFS, and optionally minted as a token to prove origin and integrity.

Product Specs

  • 5" capacitive touchscreen
  • Raspberry Pi 4B based architecture
  • Embedded camera and microphone
  • WiFi & Bluetooth with cryptographic ESP32 security chip
  • OLED feedback display for recording state
  • Supports IPFS uploads, metadata QR generation, and smart contract minting
