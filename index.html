Why Tokenize? And Why Now?

In an age where AI can clone a voice or generate a face in seconds, the line between truth and fiction is blurrier than ever. Deepfakes aren’t just a curiosity—they’re a threat to public trust, journalism, and identity itself.

For streamers, creators, and journalists, tokenization means more than ownership—it means traceability. With blockchain-based authentication, your work carries an unforgeable signature that says, "This is real. This is mine."

Think of it as a manifesto for media integrity: a declaration that what you captured, created, or broadcast wasn’t just content—it was truth, and arcHIVE makes sure it stays that way.
